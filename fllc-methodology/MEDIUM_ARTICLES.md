## Learning Strategy

1. **Foundation** — Complete Beginner + Pre Security paths
2. **Choose Track** — Offensive (pentest) or Defensive (SOC/IR)
3. **Practice** — Do 2-3 rooms per day, take notes
4. **Write** — Document every room in your own words
5. **Build** — Create your own tools based on what you learn
6. **Certify** — Use THM paths to prep for industry certs
7. **Contribute** — Write Medium articles about your journey
